MYSTERIES & ENIGMAS

On December 4, 1872, the Mary Celeste was found in working condition, but inexplicably abandoned by her crew. The fate of the crew and their reason for abandoning ship remain unknown to this day.

On January 25, 1959, a group of hikes set out on a trek across the Ural mountains, led by Igor Dyatlov. All members od the expedition died under seemingly mysterious circumstances.

On April 18, 1943, four boys discovered a body in the hollow trunk of a wych elm in Hagley Wood. The following year, graffiti reading 'Who put Bella down the Wych Elm?' appeared on a wall in Upper Dean Street, Birmingham.
